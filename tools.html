Mental Health Tools

1. Self-Assessment

Check in with yourself regularly using simple questions:

  • How am I feeling emotionally right now?
  • What do I need at this moment?
  • Am I overwhelmed, tired, content, or anxious?

2. Grounding Technique (5-4-3-2-1)

Reconnect with the present moment:

  • 5 things you can see
  • 4 things you can touch
  • 3 things you can hear
  • 2 things you can smell
  • 1 thing you can taste

3. Affirmations

Repeat gently to yourself:

  • “I am grounded and in control.”
  • “My emotions are valid.”
  • “I am doing the best I can.”

4. Box Breathing

Use this technique to calm your nervous system:

  • Inhale for 4 seconds
  • Hold for 4 seconds
  • Exhale for 4 seconds
  • Hold for 4 seconds
  • Repeat 4+ rounds

5. Sensory Toolkit

Gather calming sensory items:

  • Soft fabric or cozy blanket
  • Lavender essential oil or calming tea
  • Fidget item or textured object
  • Favorite music or white noise
