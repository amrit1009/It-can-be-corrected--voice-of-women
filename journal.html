Journaling Prompts for Reflection & Healing

  • What do I want right now, in this season of life?
  • What makes me feel most like myself?
  • When was the last time I felt truly at peace?
  • What does healing look like for me?
  • How do I speak to myself — and how can I be kinder?
  • What parts of my past am I still carrying?
  • What am I proud of, even if no one else noticed?
  • What feels heavy right now?
  • What do I want to forgive — in myself or others?
  • What is something small that brought me joy recently?
  • If I could say one thing to my younger self, what would it be?
  • What boundaries do I need to protect my peace?
  • What do I wish more people knew about me?
  • What does ‘enough’ feel like?
  • How do I respond when I feel triggered?
  • What role does rest play in my life?
  • What kind of life feels most authentic to me?
  • How do I define success — truly?
  • What am I learning to let go of?
  • What am I deeply grateful for today?

✨ These prompts are for your quiet moments. There are no wrong answers. Please feel free to share your thoughts with us on:- https://amrit1009.github.io/Itcanbecorrected./share.html
