Recommended Healing Books

The Body Keeps the Score

by Bessel van der Kolk

An essential read on how trauma affects the brain and body — and how to heal from it.

Untamed

by Glennon Doyle

A powerful memoir about finding your voice, embracing your truth, and breaking free.

What Happened to You?

by Oprah Winfrey & Dr. Bruce Perry

Shifts the question from "What's wrong with you?" to "What happened to you?" — a trauma-informed approach to understanding behavior.

Radical Acceptance

by Tara Brach

Teaches how to embrace yourself and your life with compassion and mindfulness.

Healing the Shame that Binds You

by John Bradshaw

Explores how toxic shame can hold us back, and how to break free with self-compassion.
