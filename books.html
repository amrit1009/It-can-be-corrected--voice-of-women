Recommended Healing Books

I Decided to Live as Me

by Kim Soo-Hyun

Adeeply introspective and affirming book about embracing your true self in a world full of expectations.

The Alchemist

By Paulo Coelho

A timeless journey of destiny and following your dreams, filled with wisdom and inspiration.

Ikigai

By Héctor García & Francesc Miralles

Explore the Japanese concept of purpose and how to live a meaningful, long life.

The Happy Prince

By Oscar Wilde

A short but powerful story about sacrifice, kindness, and the soul's true value.

The Little Prince

By Antoine de Saint-Exupéry – A tender story that explores love, loss, and what it means to see with the heart. A timeless read for all ages.
